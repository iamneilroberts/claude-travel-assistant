Voygent Quick Reference

AI-Powered Travel Proposal Assistant

Getting Started

Works through ChatGPT, Claude, or other AI chat clients. Just describe what you need.

"Create a trip for the Smiths - Portugal, 10 days in March"

Managing Trips

"Show me all my active trips"
"Update the Henderson trip - change hotel to Hotel Lungarno"
"Add activities: wine tasting, cooking class, sunset sailing"

Tiered Pricing

Present Value, Premium, and Luxury options side-by-side.

"Add three tiers: budget $4K, mid-range $6K, luxury $10K"

Adding Photos

Upload via web link (paste, drag & drop, or browse).

"Add a photo of Hotel Marincanto" → click link → upload → "done"
"Show me my uploaded photos" → opens gallery

Maps & Videos

Proposals include Google Maps (inline with hotels, activities) and YouTube videos.

"Add a YouTube video about things to do in Santorini"
"Include maps for each hotel location"

Publishing

"Preview the proposal"
"Publish and send to the client"

Includes: interactive maps, videos, client comments, QR code, PDF-ready formatting.

Trip Validation

Catch problems before your client does.

"Validate the Henderson trip"
ChecksLodging gaps, impossible timing, missing info, weather concerns
ReturnsCritical issues, warnings, suggestions, strengths

Quote Readiness

"Quote check - what's missing to book this?"
"Generate hand-over summary"

Shows: ready to quote, needs research, open questions, next priority.

Import Confirmations

Paste booking confirmations to update trips with real data.

"Import this cruise confirmation" [paste email]

Extracts: confirmation #s, actual prices, dates. Flags discrepancies.

Profitability Analysis

"Analyze profitability for the Greece trip"
"I want to earn $800 on this - what should I add?"
CommissionsCruise 10-18%, Hotels 10-15%, Tours 10-20%, Insurance 20-35%
SuggestsInsurance, upgrades, transfers, pre/post nights

Client Feedback & Support

"Check for new comments on my trips"
"Send a support message about the booking issue"

Quick Commands

"my trips" List all
"validate" Check issues
"quote check" Ready to book?
"profitability" Commissions
"import quote" Parse confirmation
"preview" See draft
"publish" Go live
"hand-over" Booking summary
"comments" Client feedback
Example Workflow: Inquiry → Booking
  1. "Create trip for the Wilsons - Greek Islands, May 15-25" → validate → profitability (shows $650, suggests insurance +$180)
  2. "Add photo of Santorini hotel" → upload → preview → publish
  3. Client approves → book → "Import this confirmation" [paste] → hand-over summary
